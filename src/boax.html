Multiplayer Game

Boax is a multiplayer game that combines elements of bomberman and Super Smash Bros. The game utilizes Photon for networking, allowing players to compete against each other in real-time battles. Players must strategically place bombs and outmaneuver their opponents to be the last one standing.
